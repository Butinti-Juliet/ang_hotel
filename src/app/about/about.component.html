Butinti hotel- The peaceful place in the world

Located in the heart of Africa's Old Quarters, Butinti hotel are tailor-made for guests who are architecture enthusiasts nor tourist . Only one minute walking to hustle-and-bustle Hoan Kiem Lake area but Butinti hotel offers guests the unparalleled feelings of the utmost peace and warm stay. While the guests step out of the beautiful rooms, they can get the excitement in the air from the vibrant Rooftop Restaurant & Bar, discover untold stories with Africa Experience Tour or work out at our exclusive Gym boutique class. What differentiates Butinti hotel from others is the commitment to bring our valued guests the dedicated services, delivered by our attentive and reliable staffs.
